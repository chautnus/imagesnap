## FAQ

### Does ImageSnap replace my Google Sheet?

No. ImageSnap writes directly into your Google Sheet. It replaces the copy-paste process, not the spreadsheet.

### Can I keep my existing Sheet columns?

Yes. You define your own fields — ImageSnap captures into the structure you set up. Add columns for project name, rating, supplier, status, or anything else you need.

### What if I need fields that aren't on the product page?

ImageSnap auto-fills what it can from the page. For custom fields like internal notes, rating, or project tags — you add those manually in your Sheet or through the extension's context fields.

### Is my data stored on ImageSnap servers?

No. Images go to your Google Drive. Context goes to your Google Sheet. ImageSnap doesn't store your research data.

### How is this different from a web scraper?

ImageSnap works inside your browser while you browse normally. You choose what to capture. It's human-guided, not automated. No bots, no crawling, no policy risk.
